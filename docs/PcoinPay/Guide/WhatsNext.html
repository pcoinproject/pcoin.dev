(4) What's Next?

After you've connected your wallet to your PCOINPay, you can explore various built in tools in the software. See the full list of Use Cases here.

Creating the Point of Sale App​

PCOINPay has a PoS app which you can use to receive payments from your customers directly or to receive donations. To create the POS app, you need to have a store created in PCOINPay. View PoS instructions here.

Creating the Crowdfund App​

Create a crowdfunding campaign using PCOINPay. Unlike traditional crowdfunding platforms, the creator of the campaign is the owner of the platform. Funds go directly to the creator’s wallet without any fees. View Crowdfund instructions here.

Creating the Payment Request​

Create custom invoices which can be shared with others, by sending a link to your payment request. Users can pay the request at any time. PCOINPay automatically updates the exchange rate at the time of payment. View Payment Request instructions here.

Creating the Pay Button​

Pay buttons are great when you have a fixed amount for a product or a donation. You can easily embed the button into HTML. When the customer or a visitor clicks on the button, PCOINPay displays a checkout page and an invoice for them. View Pay Button instructions here.

Connecting your PCOINPay store to your e-commerce platform​

Depending on the CMS you're using, you can easily connect PCOINPay to your online store. Currently, PCOINPay offers following integrations :

  • WooCommerce
  • Shopify
  • Magneto
  • PrestaShop
  • Custom integration

Connecting Additional PCOINPay Services​

Additional free, open-source plugins can be used with your PCOINPay.

  • LibrePatron
  • PCOIIN Transmuter

LibrePatron is a self-hosted, censorship-free, Patreon alternative. LibrePatron uses PCOINPay Server to receive Bitcoin/PCOIN payments on a recurring basis. Funds go directly to the creator's wallet.

PCOIN Transmuter is a self-hosted service that allows you to automate your money and other tasks using PCOINPay. Create custom, automated processes using crypto service triggers based on PCOINPay. Common use-cases are fiat integration, payment forwarding and email automation. Open lightning channels, detect payments and more, using blockchain data from your own nodes in PCOINPay. View PCOIN Transmuter documentation here.

Join The PCOINPay Community​

PCOINPay Server is an open-source project, not a company. We rely on a network of diverse contributors and users to provide support for numerous use-cases. Join us in improving, learning, and building PCOINPay.

If you are a developer take a look at the Local Development guide and help us with any open issues on Github.
